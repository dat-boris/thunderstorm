Profiles - Backstage

Albert Wu (Director)

Participated in many drama and play in Chinese High School and Universities(1983-1997).  Acted in various Thunderstorm productions as Chou Pu Yuan, Ping and Chung.
Stage Experience: (1983-1997)

·          "The Thunderstorm" - Co-Director (1987), Director (1992, 1997)

·          "The greatest King of Tang Dynasty" - Co- Director (1987)

·          "Romeo and Juliet" - Director (1993)

·          "The Sunrise" - Director (1994)


Reasons for Albert to be director:

·          Joined to experience great dynamic of people in emotion.

·          Get prepared for my last term elective subject : Art Management.

·          Encouraged by my wife to do something when I am free of MBA courses in Cambridge and living in Oxford without too much academic pressure.

·          One way out instead of being a chef at home. Gain a sort of sense of achievement.

·          Resume something excited me in university memory except those romantics with others.

Fun during production:  Fashion show of Lu's family and Chou's family as part of warm-ups in Sunday rehearsal. Quote: “I love you all.”

Hui Min Ng (Stage Manager)

St Edmund Hall, 1st year PPP (Psychology, Physiology and Philosophy)

This is my third time as a stage manager, but in Oxford, it is my first play. This production is quite of a challenge to me, as being in Oxford for only a term so far, i've not yet build up any contacts, whereas back in Singapore, having acted and worked in productions for several years, things were definitely much easier. I hope that this play will be a success, and all goes well

Run Ye (Stage Manager)

I am a JYA Visiting student studying  PPE at Mansfield College. Originally from Shanghai, I studied at Bowdoin College, Maine, USA.  Thunderstorm is my first drama experience. I work with Hui Ming on backstage. I joined initially out of curiosity... and I am glad that this is turning out to be lot of fun... can't wait to see the final production.

 

Yuki Watabe (Lighting Designer)

St.John’s College, 3rd year Mathematician

At Oxford he has appeared in Honk! (OFS), produced the Bald Prima Donna (St. John's) and co-produced the OUDS New Writing Festival 2001 as well as directed Busu (Best Director, Cuppers 99), Kamabara (Wadham) and Kanawa (Wadham and Edinburgh).  He was Treasurer for St. Johns Mummers and Education Officer for OUDS last year and has just had a touring production to Cherwell School recently.  He loves life without stage though and this is probably the first and the last production as a finalist.

Yuntao Tang (Lighting Operator)

Oxford Brookes University 1st year Business Economics
This is the first time of me to attend a play. I will try my best to do my own job well

Nathanael Lim (Sound Operator)

1st year Oxford PPEist

He has been involved in previous productions both as cast and crew back in his home country of Singapore. Extremely deprived of the opportunity to make noise by friends who obviously do not appreciate his oral talents, he is thrilled to be in a situation where his audience, for once, cannot tell him to shut up.

Ga Lok Chung (Props Manager)

Reading Engineering Science at Pembroke College. I was part of a Science and Technology show in Southampton, this is the first time I have been involved in a play since Junior school. I was roped in during an APS meeting but I have found it fun learning about the hard work that goes into a production and working with great people.
