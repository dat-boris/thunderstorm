Basic Story Line

The Thunderstorm is an entangled family history played out in what turns out to be a tragic ending. with its background in the 1920s and 1930s.

30 years ago, a son of a wealthy family, Chou Puyuan, had an affair with the family maid, Shi Ping, and she gave birth of two sons. In the night before New Year, after Chou Puyan chose to marry a wealthy woman Fan Yi, his family kept the eldest son Chou Ping and he drove Shi Ping out from his with the younger dying son Dahai.

Chou Puyuan became an autocratic and cold-blooded owner of his coal mines and was living in Tianjin when he finished his study in Germany. Chou Puyuan and Fan Yi had their youngest son Chou Chung and later brought the eldest son Chou Ping to the family for reunion. Shi Ping, having been saved from the attempt of suicide and experienced bitter marriage again, married a butler, Lu Kuei, and they had a daughter, Si Feng. Fan Yi couldn't tolerate the patriarchy in the family and fell in love with Chou Ping secretly, while the latter was in secret love with servant Si Feng. But Chou Chung also loved Si Feng.

In a day before thunderstorm of summer, Shi Ping came from a distant city to see her daughter Si Feng, Fan Yi asked her to have a talk about dismissing Si Feng from her servant work. Accompaning Shi Ping's visit to Chou's house, Da Hai, now a miner and representative of labor union came to fight with his father Zhou Puyuan, who he had no clue on connection to the family. When she met Chou Puyuan again Shi Ping realized that she had come to a family where she had been driven out. Chou Ping stroke Da Hai for his straight pinpoints of the money-grabbed Chou Puyuan's bloody threat to the miners and the trick in destroying the union.

When Fan Yi, the stepmother, was convinced that Chou Ping would elope with Si Feng despite of her pleading to take her as well, her mind was bent on revenge, In a fit of hysteria, she revealed Shi Ping, Si Feng and her son Chou Chung that she had been Ping's mistress. When her son Chou Chung did not follow her to be fanned jealousy into heart to blame Ping and Feng. In last struggle, Fan Yi took Chou Puyuan down to stop the elopement.
